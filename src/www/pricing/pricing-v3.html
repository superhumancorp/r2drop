Frequently Asked Questions

How does the subscription work?

Orvimo offers flexible monthly and yearly plans, allowing you to choose the best option for your business needs. Each subscription provides access to the platform's core automation features, ensuring you can streamline your operations effectively. Depending on the plan you select, you'll receive a specific number of credits, which can be used to enhance your workflows and access premium services. This flexibility ensures that whether you're a startup or a large enterprise, you can find a plan that fits your requirements and budget.

What are credits used for?

Credits in Orvimo serve as a versatile currency within the platform, enabling users to unlock a range of advanced features and services. These credits are essential for executing workflows, which can vary in complexity and scale depending on your business needs. Additionally, credits are used to access the AI Workflow Builder, a powerful tool that allows for the creation of sophisticated automation processes tailored to your specific requirements. By utilizing credits, users can also tap into premium resources and services, ensuring that their workflows are not only efficient but also innovative and cutting-edge.

Can I add multiple users to my workspace?

Yes, you can add multiple users to your workspace. All plans offer unlimited users, allowing you to collaborate with your team seamlessly. This feature is designed to enhance teamwork and productivity, ensuring that everyone in your organization can contribute to projects without any restrictions. Whether you're a small team or a large enterprise, the ability to add unlimited users means you can scale your operations efficiently.

Do you support custom development or integrations?

Yes, we support custom development and integrations. Our platform is designed to be flexible, allowing you to tailor solutions to meet your specific business needs. Whether you require bespoke workflows or unique integrations, our team is ready to assist you in achieving your goals.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can easily upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Simply navigate to your account settings and select the plan that best suits your current needs. Our flexible options ensure you can adjust your subscription as your business evolves.
